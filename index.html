I'm a second-year Master's student in Computer Science at Rutgers University, where I'm fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dimitris N. Metaxas. I also have the privilege to work with Prof. Chengzhi Mao.

My research goal is to build more explainable and reliable intelligent systems. My primary focus lies in machine learning and natural language processing, especially understanding how large language models reason and respond to structured guidance.

My recent research interests and current projects include:
• Controllable generation and its applications
• Robustness of controlled LLMs under manipulation
• Mechanistic interpretability

I’m also open to potential collaborations on related topics. Feel free to drop me an email.
